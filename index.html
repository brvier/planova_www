Your Sync. Your Choice.

No online account. No servers. No hidden services.

Planova deliberately avoids built-in sync. Why? Because you should choose how and where your data moves.

Use Nextcloud, Dropbox, Syncthing, Git, local backups, or any method you trust. Planova works harmoniously with your existing syncing tools : without interfering or imposing.

Future-Proof by Design

Plaintext is the ultimate format for digital longevity. No matter how apps evolve, how platforms change, or how companies come and go, your data remains accessible.

With Planova, your productivity tools won't age : because plaintext never does.

Take Back Control of Your Digital Life

Planova gives you a calm, stable, private space to think, plan, and create : without subscriptions, without cloud lock-in, and without losing the freedom to shape your workflow.
